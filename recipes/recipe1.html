Payasam

lets see how to make it

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of Vermicelli roasted
  • 1/2 cup of ghee
  • 1/2 cup of roasted cashews

Steps

  1. Boil the milk and sugar together until it thickens.
  2. Add the roasted vermicelli and ghee to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the roasted cashews and let it cool.
  4. Serve hot or chilled.
