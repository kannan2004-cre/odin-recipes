Veg Kurma

lets see how to make it

Ingredients

  • Vegetables of your choice
  • Water
  • Masala
  • Salt as required
  • Oil
  • Curry leaves
  • Coconut Milk

Steps

  1. Boil all the vegetables until they are perfectly cooked.
  2. In a pan add oil and add boiled veggies and add a bit water.
  3. Add the masala and saute them.
  4. Add coconut milk and dont let it boil after a few minutes serve it hot.
