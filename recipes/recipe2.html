Kadala Curry

lets see how to make it

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of soaked kadala(chickpeas)/li>
  • Water
  • Masala
  • Salt as required
  • Mustard
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Curry leaves
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Add oil and saute mustard with onion,tomatoes and curry leaves
  2. After the ingredints are sautes add salt and masal and saute them as well.
  3. Boil the kadal and add it to the masal we prepared along with boiled water.
  4. Boil for a while until the neede consistency is required.Serve hot.
